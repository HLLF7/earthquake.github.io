1.Wang Rui lost his right leg

In 2008,15-year-old Wang Rui lost his right leg in a 2008 Sichuan earthquake accident. Later, Wang Rui got a prosthetic leg and stood up again. Encouraged by her family, she became a table tennis player. With almost no foundation, she fought her way into the national team and eventually won the women's team table tennis title with her teammates at the Tokyo Paralympic Games. “I am a survivor of the 2008 Sichuan earthquake. Thanks to table tennis for giving me a second life, I have finally fulfilled my dream of becoming an Olympic champion and won glory for my country,” she said in an interview after the match!

3. "Tough pig" goes home

On May 12, the“Pig strong” specimen was put on display at the Jianchuan Museum in Chengdu, Sichuan Province. Zhu Jianqiang, a pig from the family of Wan Xingming, a villager, Longmen Mountain town, Pengzhou, was buried in the 2008 Sichuan earthquake for 36 days and miraculously survived by eating charcoal and drinking rainwater. Jianchuan Museum director buy the pig, and gave him the nickname“36 baby” happy spent the rest of his life in the museum. Every day, countless tourists come to visit it. The 14-year-old pig was 2021 out of the Jianchuan Museum in Sichuan Province on June 16 after dying of old age.
